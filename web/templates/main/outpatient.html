ABOUT THE FREMO HOSPITAL OUT-PATIENT CENTRE

NOW OPENED: OPERATION HOURS - 24/7

The Fremo Out-patient Centre was opened in the Kawangware outskirts of Nairobi to reach out to the neighboring community, Nairobi residents and an institutions for better medical healthcare.

The Hospital anticipates that this clinics will serve to provide easy access to quality healthcare services for our customers and we would like to assure you that the services offered at this centre are of high level.
